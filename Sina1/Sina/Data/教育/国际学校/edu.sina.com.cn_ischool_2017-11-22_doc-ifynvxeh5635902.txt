　　1. Improve your English – by far the best preparation for the CAI is improving your overall English language ability in speaking, listening and writing.　　全面提高您的英语能力——在听说写方面全面增强你的英语能力，有益于你更好的为CAI面试做好准备。　　2. Actively explore your interests – We will know if what you’re saying is genuine or not so it would benefit you to truly and actively explore your academic, social or other personal interests, whatever they may be.　　积极开拓您的兴趣爱好——我们能够分辨您所说的是否真实，因此积极地开拓您在学术，社会以及其他方面的一些兴趣爱好，对您的面试表现会有所帮助。　　3. Have a conversation with someone in English before you arrive. When switching between languages, it can sometimes take time to adjust. Warm up for the interview by speaking and thinking in English before you arrive.　　在面试前用英语与他人交流——语言的转换过程往往需要一定适应的时间。在面试前不妨做个小热身，用英语与他人交流，提前进入英语的语言环境。　　4. Role-play interviews – Role plays are a good way to get used to speaking about yourself, and articulating your interests, values, & opinions.　　模拟面试——参加面试前不妨自己进行一个虚拟面试，以适应英语语言的思维习惯，同时使得自己能更自如地用英语表述个人的兴趣爱好、价值观、意见等。　　5. Read English newspapers and other English publications. You may be asked to give your opinion on a topic and it would be great if you could mention a relevant example from something you had read.　　阅读英语报章杂志——当您需要对一个话题进行讨论的时候，您能从平时的阅读积累中找到话题所相关的实例。　　　　　　责任编辑：实习生大甜
